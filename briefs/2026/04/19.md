## 💡 Tip of the Day

If your agent stack touches sensitive data, treat network egress policy like a seatbelt, not a “nice to have.”

```toml
[sandbox.network]
deniedDomains=["*.pastebin.com","*.temp-mail.org","*.anonfiles.com"]
```

Bonus move: include a quick “who called what” tool log in CI artifacts so compliance evidence is not a panic project later.
