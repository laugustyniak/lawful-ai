## 💡 Tip of the Day
If your legal/research prompts keep losing context, force a longer prompt-cache TTL and regain your sanity:

```bash
# keep context warm longer for heavy legal workflows
export ENABLE_PROMPT_CACHING_1H=1

claude
/tui fullscreen
/focus
```
