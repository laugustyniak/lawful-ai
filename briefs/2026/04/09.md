## 💡 Tip of the Day

Treat auditability like a feature, not paperwork.

```bash
# tiny compliance breadcrumb trail for AI-assisted coding
mkdir -p .ai
git diff --name-only > .ai/changed_files.txt
printf "%s | model=%s | task=%s\n" "$(date -Iseconds)" "claude/codex" "${TASK_ID:-daily-brief}" >> .ai/audit.log
```

Why it slaps: one small habit gives you traceability for **EU AI Act**, accountability for **GDPR**, and provenance breadcrumbs for copyright/IP reviews.
