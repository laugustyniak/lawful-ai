## 💡 Tip of the Day
If you want legal-grade auditability without slowing dev speed, add a pre-patch compliance guard:

```toml
# codex config.toml
[hooks.pre_apply_patch]
command = "python scripts/legal_guard.py"
```

Tiny hook, huge compliance vibes. ✅
