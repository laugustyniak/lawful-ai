## 💡 Tip of the Day
When in doubt, make your AI stack auditable *by default*:

```toml
# policy-first defaults
[sandbox.network]
deniedDomains = ["*.pastebin.com", "*.anonfiles.com"]

[audit]
logToolInvocations = true
logInputHashes = true
```
