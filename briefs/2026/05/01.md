## 💡 Tip of the Day
Use risk-tiered execution profiles so legal workflows stay audit-friendly by default:

```yaml
profiles:
  legal_review:
    fs: read-only
    network: deny
  drafting:
    fs: workspace-write
    network: allowlist
    allow_domains: [eur-lex.europa.eu]
```
