## 💡 Tip of the Day
Make compliance checks a merge blocker, not a retro meeting:

```yaml
# .github/workflows/ai-compliance.yml
name: ai-compliance
on: [pull_request]
jobs:
  check:
    runs-on: ubuntu-latest
    steps:
      - uses: actions/checkout@v4
      - uses: Varshith-07/eu-ai-act-check-action@main
```
